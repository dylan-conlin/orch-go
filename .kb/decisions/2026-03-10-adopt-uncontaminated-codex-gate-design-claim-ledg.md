## Decision

**Chosen:** Adopt uncontaminated Codex gate design (claim ledger + red-team memo + claim-label pass) over contaminated four-gate design

**Rationale:** Contaminated design (fed our diagnosis) produced over-engineered four-gate system with severity codes, canonicalization tables, blind/framed passes — exhibited the same vocabulary inflation it was trying to prevent. Uncontaminated design (fed only the artifact) independently arrived at three simple artifacts: (1) claim ledger mapping assertions to type/scope/evidence/strength, (2) red-team memo listing top 5 overclaims, (3) claim-label pass tagging sentences as observed/interpreted/hypothesized/unvalidated. Simpler, independently derived, and caught problems we missed: unfalsifiable 'agent failure is harness failure' principle, genre-mixing, false compliance/coordination dichotomy, measurements not decision-grade.
