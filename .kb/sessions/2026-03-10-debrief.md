Eledath's "8 Levels" article provided the external frame — another practitioner independently arriving at harness engineering (his Level 6) and identifying coordination as unsolved (his Level 8). Codex sharpened our thread: the dangerous failure isn't bad output, it's bad output that the pipeline upgrades to "validated." **Position:** Dylan identified the tractability move: external attention isn't needed on 176 completions/day. Internal evaluation stays for hygiene. External review gates only at claim-upgrade boundaries.
