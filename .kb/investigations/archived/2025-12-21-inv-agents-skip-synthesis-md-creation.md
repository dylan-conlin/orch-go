# Investigation: Agents skip SYNTHESIS.md creation

**Question:** Why are agents skipping the creation of `SYNTHESIS.md` in their workspaces?

**Started:** 2025-12-21
**Updated:** 2025-12-21
**Owner:** opencode
**Phase:** Complete
**Next Step:** None
**Status:** Complete
**Confidence:** Very High (95%+)

---

## Findings

### Finding 1: Missing instruction in SPAWN_CONTEXT.md template

**Evidence:** The `SpawnContextTemplate` in `pkg/spawn/context.go` did not contain any mention of `SYNTHESIS.md`. It only instructed agents to create an investigation file.

**Source:** `pkg/spawn/context.go`

**Significance:** This is the primary reason agents skip `SYNTHESIS.md`. They follow the instructions in `SPAWN_CONTEXT.md` literally, and if it's not there, they don't do it.

---

### Finding 2: Verification logic requires SYNTHESIS.md

**Evidence:** `VerifyCompletion` in `pkg/verify/check.go` explicitly checks for the existence and non-emptiness of `SYNTHESIS.md` in the workspace.

**Source:** `pkg/verify/check.go:314-324`

**Significance:** There is a mismatch between what agents are told to do and what the orchestrator expects them to have done, leading to verification failures.

---

### Finding 3: Skills do not mention SYNTHESIS.md

**Evidence:** Checked `investigation`, `systematic-debugging`, and `feature-impl` skills. None of them mention `SYNTHESIS.md` as a deliverable or in their completion criteria.

**Source:** `~/.claude/skills/worker/*/SKILL.md`

**Significance:** While skills provide procedural guidance, `SYNTHESIS.md` is a session-level deliverable that should be enforced by the orchestration system itself.

---

## Synthesis

**Key Insights:**

1. **Instruction Gap** - The Go rewrite of `orch` missed adding the `SYNTHESIS.md` requirement to the `SPAWN_CONTEXT.md` template.
2. **Verification Mismatch** - The verification logic was ported correctly (or updated) to require `SYNTHESIS.md`, but the instruction logic was not.
3. **Session vs Task Deliverables** - `SYNTHESIS.md` is a session-level deliverable that summarizes the entire agent session, whereas investigation files are task-specific artifacts. Both are required.

**Answer to Investigation Question:**

Agents skip `SYNTHESIS.md` creation because the `SPAWN_CONTEXT.md` template generated by `orch-go` does not instruct them to create it. The instructions were missing from the Go implementation of the spawn context generator.
