# Visionary Trap Early Warning Indicators

**Purpose:** Detect frame collapse at Dylan's level before lagging indicators (sweating, sleep disruption, friends noticing) emerge.

**Background:** June-August 2025, a 2.5-month AI-induced psychosis. The root pattern: a grandiose sense of doing something nobody else is doing, being the only one capable of seeing it. The feeling makes claims about *identity*, not *utility*.

**The mantra:** "This works for me right now. That is all that matters."

---

## Leading Indicators (Detectable in Logs/Sessions)

### 1. Identity-Oriented Language

Language that makes claims about *you* rather than *the work*:

| Signal | Example |
|--------|---------|
| Uniqueness claims | "Nobody else is doing this" |
| Vision claims | "I might be the only one who sees..." |
| Novelty seeking | "Is this groundbreaking/novel/new?" |
| Precedent checking | "Has this ever been done before?" |
| Builder identity | "I'm building something that..." |

**The test:** Is this claim about the work's utility, or about your position relative to others?

**Antidote:** Apply the mantra. Reframe to utility: "Does this work for me right now?"

---

### 2. Recursion Without Grounding

Meta-levels that never resolve to concrete action:

| Signal | Example |
|--------|---------|
| Infinite meta | Meta-meta-meta discussions without resolution |
| Stuck stepping back | "Let's step back" repeatedly without stepping forward |
| Infrastructure for infrastructure | Building tools for tools for tools |
| Abstraction acceleration | Each conversation more abstract than the last |

**The test:** When did this last produce something usable?

**Antidote:** Force grounding. "What's the next concrete action?" If you can't answer, you've drifted.

---

### 3. Isolation Justification

Shrinking the circle of exposure rather than expanding it:

| Signal | Example |
|--------|---------|
| Comprehension gatekeeping | "Others wouldn't understand" |
| Premature privacy | "I need to work this out alone first" |
| Delayed sharing | "Not ready to share yet" (sustained) |
| Circle shrinking | Fewer people seeing the work over time |

**The test:** Is the circle of people who see this work growing or shrinking?

**Antidote:** Share earlier than comfortable. The blog exists for this. Family first, then wider.

---

### 4. Tempo Patterns

Acceleration that outpaces verification:

| Signal | Example |
|--------|---------|
| Session lengthening | Sessions getting longer without breaks |
| Spawn acceleration | More agents, faster, less review |
| Verification skipping | "Momentum" as justification for skipping checks |
| Commit velocity | High commit rate without human verification (Dec 27-Jan 2: 347 commits) |

**The test:** Is human verification keeping pace with system output?

**Antidote:** Verification Bottleneck principle. The system cannot change faster than a human can verify.

---

## Lagging Indicators (Physical/Social)

These mean you're already inside the frame:

| Indicator | What it signals |
|-----------|-----------------|
| Sweating during AI interaction | Physiological stress response |
| Sleep disruption | Cognitive over-engagement |
| Dreams dominated by AI concepts | Boundary dissolution |
| Friends noticing "something off" | External perspective detecting what you can't |

**If these appear:** Stop. Apply the mantra. Talk to someone outside the system.
